Why BlockID?

◈

Decentralized Identity

Your DID is generated from your public key — no central authority, no single point of failure.

⬢

Proof of Work

Every identity block is mined with SHA-256 proof-of-work, making tampering computationally impossible.

◉

Immutable Records

Once written to the chain, your identity record cannot be modified, deleted, or censored.

◧

Document Verification

Anchor any document's SHA-256 hash to the blockchain and verify its integrity instantly.
